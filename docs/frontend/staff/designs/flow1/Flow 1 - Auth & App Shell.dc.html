DECISION — open question 2 (dashboard depth): recommending a thin launcher (client-side counts + links into the flow 2a/2b queues) over embedded queue previews. Counts come from the plain list endpoints with no pagination guarantee at scale, so previewing rows here means fetching and rendering the same data twice; a launcher keeps the dashboard fast and pushes triage into the purpose-built queue screens. Cost of the alternative: richer at-a-glance context, at the price of duplicated fetches and a second place row-level actions must stay in sync.
