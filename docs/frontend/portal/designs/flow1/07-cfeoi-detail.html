Blockchain Architect

Ministry of Health
Remote
2 Slots Available
Closes Oct 30, 2024

Role Overview

The Ministry of Health is seeking a highly skilled Blockchain Architect to lead the design and implementation of a secure, decentralized patient identity verification system. This initiative aims to streamline healthcare access for citizens while ensuring the highest standards of data privacy and cryptographic security. You will collaborate with cross-functional government IT teams and external cybersecurity advisors to build a scalable architecture that integrates with existing national health databases.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and document the end-to-end blockchain architecture for the patient identity network.
  • Evaluate and select appropriate consensus mechanisms and smart contract frameworks suitable for public sector healthcare data.
  • Develop proof-of-concept prototypes to validate security models and performance scaling.
  • Provide technical leadership and mentorship to internal development teams during the implementation phase.
  • Ensure compliance with national data protection regulations and healthcare data standards.
